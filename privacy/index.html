Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This is the website for My Most Mostest, the children's picture book by Brie Hollingsworth Krebs, illustrated by Elle Harting. We want you to feel good about visiting, so here's a plain-language rundown of what information we collect and how we use it. Questions? Email us any time at info@mymostmostest.com.

The short version: we use Google Analytics to understand how the site is doing, and we only receive your details if you choose to write to us or sign up for updates. We don't sell your personal information, and we don't run advertising trackers.

Information we collect

Automatically, through analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to see how people find and use the site. It collects things like which pages you view, which buttons you tap (for example, clicking through to a retailer, signing up for updates, or sending us a message), your approximate (city-level) location, and your device and browser type. This is done with small analytics cookies (named _ga and similar). Google does not report your full IP address to us, and we use this data in aggregate — to learn what's resonating, not to identify you.

Information you give us

  • Contact form ("say hello"): the name, email, and message you send, so we can read and reply.
  • Email sign-up: your email address, if you ask to hear about new releases and events.

How we use it

  • To understand what visitors enjoy and improve the site.
  • To read and respond to messages you send us.
  • To send occasional updates — only if you signed up, and you can unsubscribe any time.

Cookies

Cookies are small files a site stores in your browser. We use them only for the analytics described above — no advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. You're in control: you can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, and you can opt out of Google Analytics everywhere by installing Google's Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third parties

A few trusted services help the site run:

  • Google Analytics — website analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • FormSubmit — delivers your contact-form and sign-up submissions to our inbox.
  • Retailer links — buttons to Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Bookshop.org, and Walmart take you to those stores, which have their own privacy policies.

We do not sell your personal information.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for parents, caregivers, teachers, and other grown-ups who buy and share books. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • Opt out of analytics using your browser settings or the opt-out add-on linked above.
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time.
  • Email info@mymostmostest.com to ask what we have about you, or to have it deleted.

Depending on where you live (for example, the EU/UK or California), you may have additional rights over your personal data. Just reach out and we'll help.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle information, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.
